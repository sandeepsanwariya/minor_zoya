WELCOME TO ZOYA

As humans, we want to be a part of a community, we like to support great art, great creators, great products

On Zoya, Creators can START AN PRIVATE ONLINE COMMUNITY, share their best photos, videos, blog posts or anything you like.

The main idea is creators or businesses can charge a fixed monthly membership fee from their supporters to join the club.

BENEFITS of a private online club for supporters and shops/creators:

Creators-
  • NO COMPANIES MAKING MONEY FROM YOUR ART, COMPLETELY INDEPENDENT
  • Build a strong support that is there for you and not what some website thinks
  • Make art that you want! No clickbait, no fakeness
  • No risk of demonetization
Shops-
  • Connect with their customers directly.
  • Provide small trials and discounts directly to club members only
  • Sell your products directly without maintaining websites
Supporters-
  • Support the art or products that you think deserve it
  • PROTECT YOUR FAVOURITE ART FROM CORPORATE MONEY
